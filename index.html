Did You Know?

About 85% of American homes have hard water, which can cost homeowners hundreds of dollars annually in extra soap, detergent, and energy costs.

Hard Water

Hard water contains high levels of dissolved minerals, primarily calcium and magnesium. These minerals are picked up as water moves through soil and rock.

Signs of Hard Water:

  • White scale buildup on faucets and showerheads
  • Soap doesn't lather easily
  • Spots on dishes after washing
  • Dry skin and dull hair after showering
  • Stiff, scratchy laundry

Effects of Hard Water

On Your Home:

Scale buildup reduces water flow in pipes, decreases water heater efficiency by up to 30%, and shortens appliance lifespan.

On Your Body:

Minerals react with soap to form a sticky residue that can clog pores, leading to skin irritation and dryness.

Chlorine in Water

Chlorine is commonly added to municipal water supplies as a disinfectant to kill harmful bacteria and pathogens. While effective for public health, it can have some undesirable effects.

Signs of Chlorinated Water:

  • Strong chemical smell from tap water
  • Eye irritation when showering
  • Bleached or faded fabrics after washing
  • Dry, itchy skin
  • Unpleasant taste in drinking water

Health Considerations

While chlorine effectively kills harmful microorganisms, long-term exposure to chlorine and its byproducts (like trihalomethanes) may be linked to certain health risks including respiratory issues and potential increased cancer risk.

Water Treatment Solutions

Water Softeners

Ion exchange water softeners replace calcium and magnesium ions with sodium or potassium ions, effectively removing water hardness.

Best for: Hard water problems

Carbon Filters

Activated carbon filters remove chlorine, bad tastes, odors, and some organic contaminants through adsorption.

Best for: Chlorine removal, improving taste

Reverse Osmosis

RO systems force water through a semi-permeable membrane, removing up to 99% of dissolved solids and contaminants.

Best for: Comprehensive contaminant removal

UV Purification

Ultraviolet light destroys bacteria, viruses and other microorganisms without adding chemicals to the water.

Best for: Microbial contamination
